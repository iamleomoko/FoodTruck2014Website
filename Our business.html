Eat 'N Run is a South African food service company founded by a group of four students, with
the aim of providing high-quality food, along with reasonable prices. We operate in the city
of Cape Town and we offer a variety of food products. We believe in being adventurous and unique
with our products, thus all food is prepared on sight and made to the customers specifications.
Our team is extremely motivated and dedicated in continually striving to improve.
We seek to put our customers first, making the experience as pleasurable as possible.

Our food trucks are also available for community events. Feel free to connect with us on
our facebook or twitter page to make a request.

We hope to see you in the streets of Cape Town!
